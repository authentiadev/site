Our background

Authentia has been building global teams since 2015. We started with talent from the Philippines and Vietnam, shipping production work for global consultancies, financial institutions, and software companies. Early-stage founders were part of the mix too, which taught us to work with clients at every stage of scale and clarity.

In 2018 we started applying machine learning to how we screen and match talent. That work shaped how we think about combining AI with human expertise, and it's the foundation of what we offer today. The specialists we recommend to clients are the same ones we use to run Authentia itself.
