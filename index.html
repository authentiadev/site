Our people hiring process

Whether you need one or two pinch hitters to lighten the load or an entire team to build the digital product of your dreams, at Authentia we can help with any project, great or small

01

Let's chat!

We'd love to get to know you and find out what jobs you're looking to fill.

Our origin story

Authentia began building remote teams in 2015. We worked closely with Philippine and Vietnamese talent and shipped real products into production for global consultancies, major financial institutions and established software firms. We also partnered with early stage founders which helped us build range and learn to adapt to clients of all sizes and all levels of clarity.

Along the way we saw patterns in how teams hired and how decisions were made which led us to experiment with AI early. In 2018 we began applying machine learning to talent screening and matching and that opened the door to everything we are doing now. Today we still work with offshore specialists to deliver outcomes for clients and we also use those same offshore specialists inside Authentia itself which keeps us grounded in the same reality as the companies we serve.

People roles
